Modeled vibration-induced response coupled with piezoelectric transducers in Ansys Mechanical. Performed structural–electromechanical coupling, optimized energy harvesting geometry, and documented results for grant-funded research.

Tools: Ansys Mechanical · MATLAB

Numerical Analysis of Air Film Cooling (OpenFOAM)

FOSSEE Research Migration

Implemented film cooling simulations on a 2D flat plate using OpenFOAM v2312 with buoyant solvers for conceptual study. Validated adiabatic film-cooling effectiveness against Li & Wang (reference), used blockMesh with grading for boundary-layer resolution, and explored blowing ratio and mainstream temperature effects.

Tools: OpenFOAM · ParaView · MATLAB
Project page

Effect of Hole Geometry on Film Cooling Effectiveness

Compressible flow simulations · OpenFOAM

Studied the influence of coolant hole geometry (cylindrical vs fan-shaped) on adiabatic film cooling for subsonic regimes using k-ε turbulence modeling. Evaluated effectiveness, CRVP formation and vortex dynamics.

Tools: OpenFOAM · Post-processing (ParaView, MATLAB)
