Numerical Analysis of Air Film Cooling (OpenFOAM)

FOSSEE, IIT Bombay — OpenFOAM migration project

Reproduced film-cooling results from literature using OpenFOAM v2312 and validated against ANSYS Fluent. Implemented blockMesh, buoyantBoussinesqSimpleFoam for conceptual study, and post-processed results with ParaView and MATLAB. Investigated blowing ratio, injection angle and mainstream temperature effects. Project page: FOSSEE project.

Effect of Hole Geometry on Film Cooling Effectiveness

OpenFOAM — Compressible flow simulations

Studied how hole geometry affects film cooling performance in subsonic regimes. Simulations used k-ε turbulence model and validated against experimental data; fan-shaped holes improved effectiveness.
